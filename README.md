## 🧩 Projects Overview

1. **🧹 Data Cleaning** – Handle missing values, duplicates, and outliers in a simulated dataset.  
2. **🔍 EDA** – Explore data distributions, summary statistics, and correlation heatmaps.  
3. **📈 Linear Regression** – Predict sales from advertising spend.  
4. **🤖 Logistic Regression** – Simulate and predict customer churn.  
5. **📊 Hypothesis Testing** – Perform t-tests to evaluate differences between groups.  
6. **👥 Clustering (K-Means)** – Segment customers based on age and income.  
7. **📊 Visualization Dashboard** – Interactive bar, line, and histogram visualizations.
